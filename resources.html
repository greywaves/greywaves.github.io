UK Showdown

You or your club can email UK Showdown to request new equipment.
If you order equipment yourself, you're responsible for the purchase, delivery and any returns.

Bats

Slovakian-made Showdown bats (Opens in new tab) are very popular. With prices ranging from €10 to €130, there is a bat to suit most players.
You can contact them through their Facebook page or by emailing Dusan directly.

There are other bats available from Belgium with 3-D printed handles. Ask UK Showdown for details.

Gloves

You must have adequate padding on your glove when you play Showdown. The back of your glove, including the fingers, must be covered.

  • Bytes Hockey Gloves (Opens in new tab) are good starting gloves.
  • Grays International Pro Hockey Gloves (Opens in new tab) are a tighter-fitting alternative.
  • TK 1 gloves (Opens in new tab) are also popular.

Wristbands - Hand and Arm Protection

Hand protection and arm protection are different. You can have additional hand protection above your glove, that must only extend to 6 cm above your wrist bone. Anything above 6 cm is considered arm protection
Hand protection must be one piece: if you use a small wristband or bandage, it needs to be sewn into the glove.

If you use more than one wristband, they can be any colour, as long as your short wristband (hand protection) is a different colour to your longer wristband (arm protection).
It's suggested that your hand protection is the same colour or a similar colour to your glove.

  • Small wristbands (Opens in new tab) can be used for hand protection above your glove, up to 6 cm above your wrist bone.
  • longer wristbands (Opens in new tab) can be used as arm protection, starting from 6 cm above your wrist bone.

Masks

Masks must completely obscure the vision of the athlete. Sleep masks are not allowed in national or international competitions.

  • Goalfix Eclipse Eye Shades (Opens in new tab) are a popular choice.
  • MK2 Eye Shades (Opens in new tab) do not have the ‘beak’ of the Eclipse.

Balls

Danish Showdown balls (Opens in new tab) are used nationally and internationally. You can buy them individually or as a pack of 12 from HandiLife Sport.
