Showdown Resources

Below are links to Showdown resources for easy accessing.
If you want anything added, you can email me.

UK Showdown

You or your club can email UK Showdown to request new equipment.
If you order equipment yourself, you're responsible for the purchase, delivery and any returns.

Bats

Slovakian-made Showdown bats (Opens in new tab) are some of the best bats on the market. With prices ranging from €10 to €130, there is a bat to suit most players.
You can contact them through their Facebook page or by emailing Dusan directly.

There are other bats available from Belgium with 3-D printed handles. Ask UK Showdown for details.

Gloves

Bytes Hockey Gloves (Opens in new tab) provide adequate padding in a variety of sizes.
Grays International Pro Hockey Gloves (Opens in new tab) are a tighter-fitting alternative.
TK 1 gloves (Opens in new tab) are also popular.

Masks

Goalfix Eclipse Eye Shades (Opens in new tab) are a popular choice for Showdown.
The MK2 Eye Shades (Opens in new tab) are slightly cheaper and do not have the ‘beak’ of the Eclipse.

Balls

Danish Showdown balls (Opens in new tab) are used nationally and internationally. You can buy them individually or as a pack of 12 from HandiLife Sport.

Miscellaneous Resources

These are resources that don’t quite fit anywhere else.

A note on wristbands: if you use more than one wristband, they can be any colour, as long as your short wristband (hand protection) is a different colour to your longer wristband (arm protection).
It's suggested that your hand protection is the same colour or a similar colour to your glove. Additional hand protection must be sewn into the glove. Ask UK Showdown for details.

Small wristbands (Opens in new tab) can be used for hand protection above your glove, up to 6 cm above your wrist bone.

longer wristbands (Opens in new tab) can be used as arm protection starting 6 cm above your wrist bone.
