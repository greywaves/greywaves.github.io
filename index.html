Showdown

This is a site for all things Showdown, created and maintained by Ellie. Use the links at the top of the page to find things.

What is Showdown?

Showdown is a table-based racket sport with similarities to table tennis and air hockey. Created by Joe Lewis and Patrick York in the 1970s, it has grown in popularity throughout the world and is now played in countries around Europe, Asia, South America and Africa.

Athletes wear blackout eye shades, use a wooden bat to hit an audible ball across the table an wear a protective glove on their batting hand. The aim of the game is to hit the ball into your opponent's goal, and block your opponent from doing the same.

Find Out More

Visit the IBSA website to find out more about Showdown globally, or the British Blind Sport website to find out more about Showdown in the UK.

The UK Showdown Facebook page is the best way to keep up to date with events and Showdown happenings in the UK!

Who is this website for?

I have gathered useful resources, a directory of clubs in the UK and more here as a quick reference point for anyone interested. Any Showdown player can use this but it is primarily aimed at centralising information for those in the UK, or people curious about Showdown.
