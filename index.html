    Showdown Resources

    Below are links to Showdown resources for easy accessing.
    If you want anything added, you can email me.

    UK Showdown

    You or your club can email UK Showdown to request new equipment.
    If you order equipment yourself, you're responsible for the purchase, delivery and any returns.

    Bats

    Slovakian-made Showdown bats are some of the best bats on the market. With prices ranging from €10 to €130, there is a bat to suit most players.
    You can contact them through their Facebook page or by emailing Dusan directly.

    There are other bats available from Belgium with 3-D printed handles. Ask UK Showdown for details.

    Gloves

    Bytes Hockey Gloves provide adequate padding in a variety of sizes.
    Grays International Pro Hockey Gloves are a more flexible, tighter-fitting alternative.

    Masks

    Goalfix Eclipse Eye Shades are a popular choice for Showdown.
    The MK2 Eye Shades are slightly cheaper and do not have the ‘beak’ of the Eclipse.

    Balls

    Danish Showdown balls are the IBSA standard for Showdown balls. You can buy them individually or as a pack of 12 from HandiLife Sport.

    Miscellaneous Resources

    These are resources that don’t quite fit anywhere else.

    A note on wristbands: if you use more than one wristband, they can be any colour, as long as your short wristband (hand protection) is a different colour to your longer wristband (arm protection).
    It's suggested that your hand protection is the same colour or a similar colour to your glove. Ask UK Showdown for details.

    Small wristbands can be used for hand protection above your glove, up to 6 cm above your wrist bone.

    longer wristbands can be used as arm protection starting 6 cm above your wrist bone.
